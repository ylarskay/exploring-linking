The Tower & Death

The Tower still represents sudden changes that are for the better in this connection.   

 

Death is not a card that is there to warn you about the literal death of a person. This card is all about transformation and change. You must change old habits and things that you no longer need, they are holding you back. This transformation will lead you to some new, something positive. Just like the Tower this change can be sudden and come as a surprise.
