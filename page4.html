The Fool & Wheel of Fortune

The Fool is innocent in spirit and often depicted on the edge of a cliff. Similarly to the Wheel of Fortune the Fool brings the opportunity to follow your heart's desire.

 

 

Wheel of Fortune is about heightened intuition. While the Fool leads you to take things into your own hands the wheel leaves changes up to fate. Don't worry, it's usually positive.
