Temperance, The Tower, & The Star 

Temperance shows the need for balance. Disharmony is brought to life by priorities that need to be re-examined. It is visually similar to the Star and leads to the Tower.

 

 

The Tower is about dismantling those disharmonies. It is all about change and destruction and taking you out of your comfort zone. This transformation is often sudden which is why the figure is depicted wearing a mask.  

 

 

The Star is the last step in this journey. It represents hope. Water is returning to the river instead of be held between two containers like in Temperance. In this stage you should feel relaxed and be able to appreciate life more.
