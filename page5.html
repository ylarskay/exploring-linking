The Sun & The World

Both the Sun and the World are pictures of success. When shown the sun card it is time to take on confidence and enjoy life. Your life and things in it are bound to improve (health, relationships, work, etc). 

 

 

The World is the end of a journey. All the effort and decisions you have made along the way have paid off, you will be rewarded. Life is balanced and will run smoothly.
